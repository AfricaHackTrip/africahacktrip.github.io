We are a group of developers and designers from Europe. When we heard about the emerging African tech hubs we decided to take a trip to connect and exchange with local communities.

We met incredible people, shared ideas and experiences and built things together. We learned about cool projects and unique challenges, visited amazing spaces, and - most importantly - made friends.

Why

We had been working with people from all over the world, but not yet with hackers from Africa. We were curious and wanted to change that.

We also wanted to share our experiences with our local communities and kick off a continuous exchange.

How

We went as self-funded individuals in order to meet at eye-level and organized barcamps and hackathons to get in touch with as many people as possible.

When/Where

In 2013 we went to East Africa and spent a week each in Nairobi (Kenya), Kampala (Uganda), Kigali (Rwanda) and Dar es Salaam (Tanzania) and visited the main tech hubs there.

AfricaHackTrip - The Movie

This 30-minutes documentary contains mostly material from the barcamps and hackathons and a lot of interviews with people we met.

You will experience the fun we had and learn about the development of the tech hubs in Africa, unique aspects of markets and products and amazing community initiatives.
